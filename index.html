About Pizza Slider

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Pizza Slider began as a small family-owned pizzeria with a passion for creating innovative pizza experiences. What started as a single location has grown into a beloved local chain, known for our rotating selection of unique pizza creations.

Our Philosophy

At Pizza Slider, we believe in:

  • Using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients
  • Innovating while respecting pizza traditions
  • Creating a fun, welcoming atmosphere for all our guests
  • Supporting our local community through various initiatives

Our Team

Our chefs bring together decades of culinary experience from around the world, combining traditional techniques with modern flair. Each month, our team collaborates to create new seasonal specials that keep our menu exciting and fresh.

Sustainability

We're committed to environmentally responsible practices:

  • 100% of our packaging is compostable or recyclable
  • We source ingredients from sustainable farms
  • Our restaurants use energy-efficient equipment
  • We donate unused food to local shelters
